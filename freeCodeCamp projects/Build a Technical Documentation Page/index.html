Introduction

Rice is one of important dishes in Southern East Asian usual meals. Especially in Vietnam, people there might eat at least one bowl of rice for each meal, from morning to evening, everyday. Rice is also an traditional material in Vietnam. And for a rice exported country, eating rice everyday likes western people eat bread and drink milk.

The content of rice is rich of powder and sugar, which give human body very much energy for work. Most foreign people think Vietnamese only eat boiled rice, but people there also make a large dish from rice, such as "phở", "bún", "bánh chưng", etc. With milk, you can make butter or cheese. With rice, you can make many more dish with creative way.

For this documentation, I will provide how to make Fried Rice. I am pretty sure it is not a hard dish to make, but most of you readers don't know my style, in other word, my recipe.

Recipe

Alright, when you have at least 2 of 3 of my requirement in previous section, let's get to recipe part to know what ingredients to cook this fried rice dish.

  • 1 bowl of cold boiled rice
  • 2 chicken eggs
  • 1 spoon of mayonaise
  • 1 coffee spoon of spice
  • 1 coffee spoon of spicy sauce
  • 1 coffee spoon of fish sauce

Let's settle these to each bowl around your stove so you can put in in your cooking instrument fast.

How it made

First, you need a pan, you can choose the size if you want. If you cook for yourself, you only need small one. If for your girl friend, use the medium one. If for you whole family, use big large pan. As this is a technical documentation, I will use some condition pseudo code to explain how to choose a pan.

pan_size = cook_for_me ? small : cook_for_girl ? medium : cook_for_family ? large : undefined

Make sure your pan is not wet by put in on stove. Heat it until you don't see any water on the pan surface. Then pour oil in. If you pour oil when there is water, you'll make firework in your kitchen. And we all know you should not do it. Let's do the code again.

pan_has_water ? heat() : pour_oil()

After both of your pan and oil is heated, pour your bowl of rice in the pan. Stir it a little to make it warm up. Don't be harsh, rice god does not want his prayers crushed inside a hot battle field. Then, drop your eggs in, make sure you remove their shells, God Rice again does not want to see his prayers with chicken shell, as chicken eats rice. And keep stiring until eggs and rice mix together, and the eggs are not raw, you don't want to get H5N1, you know.

Turn off your stove, let it be for a few minutes, then put the mixture to the serving bowl. Tada, fried rice like Vietnamese people always do. Nearly done.

Decoration
